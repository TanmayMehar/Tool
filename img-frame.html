Professional Image Frame & Border Tool

FramePro is the ultimate online tool for adding stylish frames and borders to your images. Whether you're looking to enhance your social media photos, create professional-looking portraits, or add decorative elements to your images, our tool provides a wide range of customization options.

With FramePro, you can choose from various frame styles including solid, dashed, dotted, double, and decorative borders. Adjust the frame width, color, opacity, and shadow intensity to create the perfect look for your images. Our tool also includes popular presets like Polaroid and vintage frames for quick styling.

All processing happens directly in your browser for maximum privacy and speed. No registration required - just upload, customize, and download your framed images instantly!
