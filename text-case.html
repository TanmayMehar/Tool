Professional Text Case Converter

CasePro is the ultimate online tool for converting text between different case styles. Whether you're a writer, programmer, student, or professional, our tool helps you format text exactly how you need it with just a few clicks.

Proper text casing is essential for readability, professionalism, and compliance with style guides. Our tool supports all major case styles including Sentence case, lowercase, UPPERCASE, Title Case, camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, and kebab-case.

With additional features like trimming extra spaces, removing duplicate lines, and sorting text alphabetically, CasePro is more than just a case converter - it's a comprehensive text formatting tool. All processing happens instantly in your browser with no server uploads required.
