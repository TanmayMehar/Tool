Image Format Guide

Different image formats serve different purposes. Understanding which format to use can help optimize your images for web, print, or storage.

JPEG

Best for photographs with many colors. Uses lossy compression but offers adjustable quality settings.

PNG

Ideal for images with transparency or text. Uses lossless compression but results in larger file sizes.

WebP

Modern format offering superior compression. Supported by most modern browsers but not universally.

GIF

Primarily used for simple animations. Limited to 256 colors, making it unsuitable for photographs.

Our converter tool allows you to easily switch between these formats while adjusting quality settings to find the perfect balance between file size and image quality for your specific needs.
